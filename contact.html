ABOUT US

Seed Landcare is a design and build firm focusing on landscape construction and maintenance in both commercial and residential projects throughout Southern California. Our professional team of landscape designers, horticulturists, gardeners and craftsman work with a management team of over 30 years of experience to provide clients with creative solutions, innovative designs and contemporary methods.

Kyle Johnson
| President

Through the combination of his high-level education and passion for landscape development Kyle Johnson has positioned himself as a leader in the industry. From green walls to street parklets, bio-retention basins to large-scale production Kyle has delivered and completed multiple projects throughout Southern California that address all facets of the ever-evolving building industry.

Lynne Burns
| Vice President

Lynne joined the Seed Landcare team in 2017 after a 25-year career with two of the leading landscape companies in California. In her role as Vice President, Lynne leads the growth and performance of the maintenance division.

Our Crews

Our thriving team is made up of over 50 crew members with decades of combined experience in installation and maintenance that always use the utmost care and professionalism. As a family of Seed, we are proud of our hardworking and loyal employee’s and grateful to have such superb customer service.
